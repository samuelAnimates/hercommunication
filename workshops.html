Rehearse for the world you want to be a part of.

HER Workshops play with the ways we talk about health & science. We experiment with formats. We experiment with moods. But we always link the discussion back to health equity.

Workshop Guides:

UwU Oracle Deck

This workshop is a creative activity around precision medicine and health equity. It is a specualtaive design exercise, in which participants imagine products that might exist in the futuree. It is also a role-play activity: participants play as employees at a health tech start-up. It runs about an hour. It's good for 4 to 8 small groups.

Click here for the Uwu Oracle Deck workshop guide.

Precision Medicine: TLDR

This workshop serves as an introduction to precision medicine and health equity. It asks for a lot of participation, relating the topics to everyday experiences. It runs about 1 hour. It's good for a group of 10-20.

Click here for the Precision Medicine: TLDR workshop guide.
